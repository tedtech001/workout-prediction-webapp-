Workout Routines

BMI Case 1

Monday

Cardio: 30 min running
    Warm-up: 5 min light jogging
    Main Workout: 30 min running (moderate pace)
    Cool-down: 5 min stretching (quads, hamstrings, calves)

Tuesday

Strength: Upper Body
Exercise Sets Reps
Push-ups 3 12
Dumbbell Bench Press 3 10
Bicep Curls 3 15
Tricep Dips 3 12
Plank 3 30 seconds 
Cool-down: 5 min stretching (chest, shoulders, arms)

Wednesday

Cardio: 30 min cycling
    Warm-up: 5 min light cycling
    Main Workout: 30 min moderate-intensity cycling
    Cool-down: 5 min stretching (quads, hamstrings, calves)

Thursday

Strength: Lower Body
Exercise Sets Reps
Bodyweight Squats 3 15
Lunges 3 12 per leg
Leg Press 3 10
Calf Raises 3 15
Glute Bridges 3 12 
Cool-down: 5 min stretching (quads, hamstrings, glutes)

Friday

Rest Day
